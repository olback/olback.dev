GTA Online Scripts

As we all know, Public sessions is a bitch. People are hacking and griefing.
Oh why not just make a "Friends only" or a "Invite only" session?

So what happens is your game drops the connection to the session server and you
get placed in a public session all by yourself. And yes, your friend can join the session.
So this is why this script exists.
This simple scrip just blocks a few ports on your local machine for 20 seconds.

Step-by-step guide.
1. Open up "Windows Firewall with Advanced Security".
2. Click on "Outbound Rules".
3. Click on "New Rule..." in the right panel.
4. Choose the "Port" alternative.


5. Paste these ports in the "Specific remote ports:" field. 6672, 61455, 61457, 61456, 61458


6. Make sure that you check the "Block the connection" alternative.


7. Check all three boxes.


8. Make sure to name it "gtao" (all lowercase). The script only works if it's all lowercase.


9. Almost done. Right click "gtao" and click on "Dissable".
10. And now you're all done! Your GTA Online experience will be greater than ever.
